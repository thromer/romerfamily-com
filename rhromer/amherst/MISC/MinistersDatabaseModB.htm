Listed here are the 21 ministers for whom I have good evidence of slave ownership. There are undoubtedly others for whom I have not yet found the evidence. The dates are the dates of their ministries (beginning with the year of their ordination), as well as the dates of their graduation from Harvard or Yale. 


* Suffield, now part of Connecticut, is included because until about 1750 it was a part of Massachusetts.
