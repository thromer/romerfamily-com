This is Shamek Weddle's sketch of African drum, cowrie shells, and chain, which is the basis for the wooden carving that will be installed in the PVMA museum soon.

 

This sketch has now been realized as a 24" mahogany plaque by wood carver Dimitrios Klitsas of Hampden, Mass. Here are a couple of snapshots. The first is a view of the entire plaque.
