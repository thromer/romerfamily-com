Ever since I began seriously studying slavery in western Massachusetts, I have been struck by the total lack of public recognition of the existence of slavery here. Initially at my instigation and with the strong support of the leadership of "PVMA" (the Pocumtuck Valley Memorial Association - the group that runs the museum in Deerfield and works with local history teachers and students), we have begun to take steps to rectify this situation.
